About

I am a data scientist and physicist, currently a Sr. Manager at Pinterest. We develop and deploy machine learning and statistical methodologies/tools to tackle modern data science problems, build data products, and enable data-driven/informed decision making. Before Pinterest I worked with a personal finance start-up called wallet.ai as part of a data science fellowship at Insight. My research background is in complex systems and condensed matter physics. I worked at Brookhaven National Lab on the development of novel sustainable energy technologies. More details on my PhD research can be found here. If you'd like to get in touch, see contact.
